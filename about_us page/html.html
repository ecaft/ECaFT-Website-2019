We Are ECaFT

“The Engineering Career Fair Team is a student-governed, professional organization that works to connect employers around the nation to Cornell students in technical, entrepreneurial, and business industries.” 

LEARN MORE JOIN US
online website builder

OUR GOALS

Empower and engage the Cornell community by connecting employers to students 

I. To provide the students of Cornell University with career opportunities through the Spring Engineering & Technical Career Fair
II. To contribute to the university and its students by hosting and planning events that supplement professional and career development.
III. To contribute to the professional growth and success of members of the team and community.
